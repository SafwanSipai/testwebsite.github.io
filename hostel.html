Hostel

A fully secured on campus hostel is the most outstanding features of this institute. With the security guards, this home away from home hostel has all facilities to make the stay comfortable and pleasant.

This is one of its kind hostel where the students are provided with an attach bathroom facility in their room itself. While on one hand this facility saves much of the time, on the other hand it serves to maintain the proper hygienic surroundings, both very essential for a growing medical student.

The college run mess provides hygienic & nutritious food to the hostel students. With comfortable cots, separate reading tables and separate cupboards; the hostel provides every little facility that a growing medical student may need.

The daily cleaning of hostel rooms & premises make it much homely. Students love to stay back and study in hostel during reading vacation which is self explanatory for their attachments with the campus.

Amenities at Hostel wing

  1. Reverse Osmosis Water Coolers
  2. Round the Clock Security
  3. Electric water geyser for hot water
  4. Canteen facility
  5. Separate Cot with Bedding
  6. Separate Cupboard, Writing Table & Chair

Punishment for Ragging

  1. Ragging or harassment of fresher's by seniors of the hostel in any form is strictly prohibited. Ragging is punishable as per the order of Supreme Court.
  2. In case of Ragging, the institute will lodge FIR against the miscreant and will be dealt as per law. Modification or addition of rules may be made and communicated as and when necessary by the authority.
