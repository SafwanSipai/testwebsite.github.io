Hospital

SHREE SARDAR PATEL MAHILA HOMOEOPATHIC MEDICAL COLLEGE & HOSPITAL situated at Hadala has its own 20 bedded full-fledged hospital with all modern facilities for routine & emergency medical care. This well-equipped teaching hospital serves as a boon to the surrounding rural locality. Various consultants both from Homoeopathy and Modern Medicine make efforts to provide primary & secondary medical aid to needful patients. Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Charitable, Medical, Educational and Research trust runs this hospital purely on charitable basis to allow its benefits to be extended to the very low income generating population.

The Hospital has its own major and minor operation theatre with an attached recovery room. The vast spacious labour room is an additional benefit for carrying out normal deliveries with ease.

The separate Male & Female general wards, Paediatrics ward, are specially designed to meet with requirement of medical care. The Surgical & Gynaecology wards are providing medical aids to the patients while simultaneously providing much needed clinical exposure to the clinical students. The hospital is equipped with modern diagnostic equipment's like, E.C.G. machines, Spriometer, well- equipped biochemistry and pathology laboratory. Almost all the medicines prescribed by the doctors are available at the dispensary counter. The outdoor and indoor patient departments are managed by team of efficient doctors, registrars, house physicians, nursing staff and paramedical personnel.

The Hospital takes up the responsibility of serving at mass level by taking medical camps at regular intervals. These camps provide diagnostics and therapeutic measures to many patients at once. The services of stalwart consultants can be availed under one roof during camp period. The Homoeopathic camps have attracted many patients from peripheral areas. The O.P.D. and I.P.D. facilities are also utilized for the clinical training of students.

Peripheral OPD

Besides our Hospital there are also the setup of two clinics in Hadala and Kagdadi, at Rajkot. The aim of the clinics is to provide better medical services to the nearby rural areas and thus for the betterment of the Health of the community. The primary treatment is provided at the clinics and for further reference they are referred to Sardar Patel Mahila Homoeopathic Medical college Hospital.
