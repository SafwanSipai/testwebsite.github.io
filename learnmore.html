About Us

SPMHMC Rajkot was founded by …………….in year 2022 with unique thought to make this institution for women’s only. The college is giving great efforts to give higher education in medical sciences for village & lower strata girls with all possible help.

SPMHMC is near the city Rajkot. It is spread inabout 2.5 acres with good infrastructure. College has well equipped departments which are as per NCH MSE norms. Besides all laboratories and library are full fledge & whatever is required for the students for the purpose of the study will definitely be provided by the trust. The trust is dedicated fully for the upliftment of educational carrier of the students, of Homoeopathic faculty.
