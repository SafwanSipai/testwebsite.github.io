Sardar Patel Mahila Homoeopathic Medical College and Hospital

Sardar Patel Mahila Homoeopathic Medical College and Hospital are located at Hadala, Rajkot-Morbi Highway road, Rajkot. The college is established with the aim to render a true and proper medical education and medical facilities to the surrounding region.

Our Values

Vision

To develop the college into a world class institution, renowned for its teaching capability, research tenacity & professional acumen by providing quality homoeopathic education to enable students to undertake responsibilities & cope with challenges, problems & opportunities in Homoeopathy through active learning & continued education with competence &concern to ensure the practice of holistic health.

Mission

To disseminate high quality education in health sciences streams among the youth & inculcate values and ethics to be practiced in the pursuit of their professions
