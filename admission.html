Admission

The process of Admission to BHMS Course starts after declaration of results of 12th (HSC) Science and NEET.

Intake capacity: 60 seats (girls only)

Eligibility Criteria: as per NCH and Govt. of Gujarat norms.

Every year the admissions are done by State Govt. through Centralized Admission committee.

Fees: According to the fee Regulatory Committee (Government of Gujarat) -

Government Quota: 95000/-

Management Quota: 125000/-
